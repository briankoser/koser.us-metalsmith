Name: Crab Quiche

Ingredients:
- 9" pie crust
- 4 eggs
- 1 c. heavy cream
- 1/2 tsp. each of salt and pepper
- 3 dashes of hot sauce
- 1 c. Monterey Jack cheese
- 1/4 c. Parmesan
- 8 oz. crab meat
- 1 green onion, chopped

Directions:
- Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Bake crust 10 minutes and allow to cool. Whisk together all other ingredients and pour in crust. Bake 25-30 minutes, then turn off oven and let sit 20-30 minutes (gives smoother texture and makes it firm).

Yield: Serves 4

Comments: Brian's favorite quiche. :)
