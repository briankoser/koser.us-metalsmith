Name: Shrimp 'n' Pasta

Tags: seafood dinner meal

Ingredients:
- 4 Tbsp. olive oil
- 4 cloves garlic, chopped
- 1/2 c. chopped onion
- 2 Tbsp. butter
- 2 lb. shrimp, peeled and deveined
- parsley and salt to taste
- 2 bags frozen whole veggies from Sam's
- 1 lb. angel hair pasta, cooked
- grated Parmesan cheese to taste

Directions:
- Heat oil in large skillet. Saute garlic and onions, but do not brown garlic.
- Add shrimp, butter, parsley and salt and saute until shrimp is pink and opaque. Meanwhile, cook vegetables in microwave. Add to shrimp right before serving. Serve over pasta and sprinkle liberally with Parmesan.

Yield: Serves 6

Comments: You may wish to add olive oil to the pasta for a little extra flavor and moisture.
