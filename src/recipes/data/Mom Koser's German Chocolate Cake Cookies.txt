Name: Mom Koser's German Chocolate Cake Cookies

Tags: dessert

Ingredients:
- 1 (18.25 oz.) pkg. pudding included German chocolate cake mix
- 1 c. semisweet chocolate chips
- 1/2 c. rolled oats
- 1/2 c. oil
- 2 eggs, slightly beaten
- chopped nuts, if desired

Directions:
- Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Combine all ingredients in large bowl. Drop by rounded teaspoonfuls 2" apart onto ungreased cookie sheet. Bake 8-10 minutes or until set. Cool 1 minute, then remove from cookie sheet.

Yield: 4 1/2 dozen

Comments: If making this at altitudes above 3500 ft., add 1/4 c. flour to dry cake mix. Bake as directed.
