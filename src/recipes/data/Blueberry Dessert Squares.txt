Name: Blueberry Dessert Squares

Tags: dessert

Ingredients:
- 1 c. flour
- 1 c. quick oats
- 1 c. brown sugar
- 1/2 tsp. salt
- 1/2 c. cold butter
- 2 1/2 c. blueberries, fresh or frozen
- 1/4 c. sugar

Directions:
- Combine flour, oats and brown sugar. Cut in butter until crumbly. Press half the mixture in greased 9" pan. Bake at 350 degrees for 10 minutes. Combine blueberries and sugar, then sprinkle over crust. Top with remaining crust mixture and press down gently. Bake 35-40 minutes. Serve warm with vanilla ice cream.

Yield: yield
