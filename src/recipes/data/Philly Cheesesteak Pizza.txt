Name: Philly Cheesesteak Pizza

Ingredients:
- 1 pizza crust
- 1/4 mayonnaise (or less)
- 1/2 lb. cooked, chopped steak
- 1/4 c. chopped sweet onion
- 1 roasted red bell pepper
- 1 1/2 c. shredded provolone cheese
- 1 1/2 c. shredded mozzarella cheese

Directions:
- Spread crust with mayo and top with remaining ingredients. Bake at 400 degrees for 15-20 minutes, or until lightly browned.

Yield: yield

Comments: Excellent flavor! If using a thin crust, you may need to bake the crust 5-10 minutes before adding the mayo and toppings.
