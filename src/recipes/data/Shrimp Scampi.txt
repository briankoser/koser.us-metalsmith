Name: Shrimp Scampi

Tags: seafood dinner fancy favorite

Ingredients:
- 3-4 garlic cloves, minced
- 1/4 c. butter
- 1/4 c. olive oil
- 1 lb. uncooked medium shrimp, peeled
- 1/4 c. lemon juice
- 1/2 tsp. pepper
- 1/4 tsp. oregano
- 1/2 c. parmesan cheese
- 1/4 c. breadcrumbs
- parsley to taste
- 12 oz. angel hair pasta, cooked

Directions:
- In a 10" *ovenproof* skillet, saute garlic in butter and oil until tender. Stir in shrimp, lemon juice, pepper and oregano; cook and stir for 2-3 minutes or until shrimp turn pink.
- Sprinkle with parmesan, breadcrumbs and parsley. Place in oven and broil 6" from heat (High) for 2-3 minutes, or until topping is golden brown. Serve over pasta.

Yield: 4 servings

Comments: Be careful not to cook the shrimp overly fast or on very high heat, or they will acquire a texture similar to pencil eraser.
