Name: French Bread Tomato Soup

Tags: dinner

Ingredients:
- 6 Tbsp. olive oil, divided
- pinch crushed red pepper flakes
- 1 loaf stale French bread, cut into cubes
- 2 cloves garlic, peeled and sliced
- 3/4 c. chopped onion
- 28 oz. diced tomatoes
- 3 tsp. dried basil
- 5 c. chicken broth
- 1 tsp. parsley
- 1/4 c. parmesan cheese

Directions:
- Heat 4 Tbsp. olive oil in large pot. Add pepper and bread and fry until lightly browned (more oil may be necessary). Remove bread to paper towels. Heat 2 more Tbsp. oil and cook garlic and onion until softened, about 5 minutes. Add tomatoes, basil and bread; cook about 10 minutes. Add remaining ingredients and bring to a boil. Reduce heat to low and simmer 20-30 minutes.

Yield: Serves 4

Comments: This is surprisingly tasty!
