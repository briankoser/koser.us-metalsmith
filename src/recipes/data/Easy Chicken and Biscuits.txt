Name: Easy Chicken and Biscuits

Ingredients:
- 1 can cream of celery
- 1 can cream of mushroom
- 1 c. milk
- 1/4 tsp. thyme
- 1/4 tsp. pepper
- 1 pkg. frozen Sam's whole veggies
- 2 c. cooked cubed chicken
- 10 oz. refrigerated biscuit dough

Directions:
- Stir all ingredients except biscuit dough together in 8x12" dish. Bake at 400 degrees for 15 minutes. Stir. Top with 7 oz. dough, then bake 15 minutes more or until biscuits are golden. Bake remaining 3 oz. dough separately to dip into sauce.

Yield: Serves 4

Comments: So easy and so tasty!
