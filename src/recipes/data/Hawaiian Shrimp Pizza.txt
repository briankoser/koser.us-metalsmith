Name: Hawaiian Shrimp Pizza

Tags: lunch party

Ingredients:
- 2 pizza crusts
- tomato salsa, or pizza sauce
- 7 oz. *cooked jumbo shrimp
- 2/3 c. small fresh pineapple chunks
- 1 red bell pepper, seeded and finely sliced
- 9 oz. mozzarella cheese
- optional garnish: fresh chopped cilantro or watercress

Directions:
- Heat oven to 425 degrees. Spread toppings on crusts in order listed. Bake 10-12 minutes, or until cheese is melting and turning golden and crusts are crisp underneath. Garnish and serve.

Yield: 2 pizzas

Comments: *If cooking the shrimp yourself, fry in butter and garlic for great flavor!
