Name: Green Beans in Bunches

Ingredients:
- 3 cans of *whole* green beans
- 1 package of bacon, but in thirds
- 1 stick butter
- 1/2 c. brown sugar

Directions:
- Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
- Wrap 5-7 beans in 1/3 of a strip of bacon; place in 9x13"dish. Continue with remaining beans and bacon.
- Melt butter and brown sugar, stirring to combine; pour over each bunch of beans and bake for 25 minutes or so.

Yield: Serves at least 6

Comments: Another Thanksgiving tradition of ours. A heart attack waiting to happen, no doubt, but it's just so good!
