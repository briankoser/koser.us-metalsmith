Name: Garlic Cheese Bombs

Ingredients:
- 1 can premade biscuits (10 pieces inside)
- 20 small cubes cheddar cheese (approx. 4 oz. total)
- 4 Tbsp. butter
- 1 clove garlic, crushed
- 1/2 c. parmesan cheese
- 3 tsp. dried parsley

Directions:
- Preheat oven to 375 degrees and line a baking sheet with parchment paper. Cut each biscuit in half and slighltly roll. Place a cheese cube in the middle; wrap dough completely around cube, sealing edges. Place butter and garlic in small bowl and melt in microwave about 12 seconds. In separate bowl, combine parmesan and parsley. Roll each ball in butter mixture, then parmesan mixture. Place on baking sheet and bake 10-12 minutes, or until just browned.

Yield: 20 biscuit balls
