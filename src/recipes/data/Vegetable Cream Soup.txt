Name: Vegetable Cream Soup

Tags: dinner

Ingredients:
- 2 Tbsp. butter
- 1/2 c. chopped onion
- VEGETABLE: 1 c. sliced celery
- 1/2 tsp. crushed HERB: dill
- 1 can condensed SOUP: cream of mushroom
- 1/2 c. cream
- 3/4 c. milk
- GARNISH: Parmesan cheese

Directions:
- Heat butter in 2-qt. saucepan. Cook onion, VEGETABLE and HERB until veggies are tender, stirring occasionally. Stir in SOUP, cream and milk. Heat through and serve with GARNISH.

Yield: Feeds 2 people
