Name: Bacon Wrapped Asparagus

Tags: side

Ingredients:
- bacon
- asparagus
- brown sugar (optional)

Directions:
- Tightly wrap several pieces of asparagus with bacon, then fry until bacon is cooked. Sprinkle with brown sugar while frying. Drain on paper towels and serve.

Yield: yield

Comments: There are no amounts because this is a very flexible recipe. Use whatever you have.
