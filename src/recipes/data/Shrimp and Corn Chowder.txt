Name: Shrimp and Corn Chowder

Tags: seafood dinner

Ingredients:
- 4 slices bacon, diced
- 1 lb. medium shrimp, peeled and deveined
- 3 cloves garlic, diced
- 1 onion, diced
- 2 tsp. smoked paprika, or more to taste
- 1/2 tsp. oregano
- 1/2 tsp. basil
- 1/4 tsp. red pepper
- salt and pepper to taste
- 4 c. chicken stock
- 2 c. corn kernels (frozen, canned or roasted)
- 1 bay leaf
- 1/4 c. heavy cream
- 2 tsp. dried parsley

Directions:
- In large stockpot over medium heat cook bacon until crisp; drain and remove to plate, reserving 1 Tbsp. bacon fat. Add shrimp to pot and cook, stirring occasionally, until pink; set aside. Add garlic and onion to pot and cook, stirring frequently, until onions have become translucent. Stir in seasonings. Whisk in chicken stock, corn and bay leaf. Bring to a boil; reduce heat and simmer until slightly thickened, about 12-15 minutes. Discard bay leaf and add cream. Puree soup with immersion blender until somewhat chunky. Stir in bacon, shrimp and parsley and serve.

Yield: Serves 5

Comments: The corn helps to thicken the chowder up!
