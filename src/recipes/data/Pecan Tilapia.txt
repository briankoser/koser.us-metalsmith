Name: Pecan Tilapia

Tags: seafood dinner fancy

Ingredients:
- tilapia fillets (4-6?)
- 1/2 c. mayo
- 3 Tbsp. grated parmesan cheese
- 1 tsp. dried basil
- 1/3 c. chopped pecans (walnuts may be substituted)

Directions:
- Preheat oven to 425 degrees, then grease a cookie sheet.
- In bowl, combine mayo, parmesan and basil. Spread over fish and sprinkle with pecans. Bake uncovered until done (10 minutes maximum).

Yield: 

Comments: This is definitely a winning recipe, and is pretty enough to be served at a fancy dinner.
