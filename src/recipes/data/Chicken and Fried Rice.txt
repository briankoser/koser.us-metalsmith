Name: Chicken and Fried Rice

Ingredients:
- 2 Tbsp. oil
- 2 cloves of garlic, diced
- chopped onion to taste
- 3 chicken breasts, diced
- salt, pepper and rosemary to taste
- 2 carrots, diced
- 1/2 c. frozen peas, thawed
- 2 eggs, beaten
- 3 c. cooked rice
- 1 1/2 Tbsp. soy sauce

Directions:
- Saute garlic and onion in oil until tender. Add chicken and spices and stir-fry until cooked. Add carrots and peas and stir-fry until tender, about 7 minutes (during last two minutes add eggs and stir constantly).
- Stir in rice and soy sauce and heat through. Serve.

Yield: 4 servings

Comments: Tasty comfort food.
