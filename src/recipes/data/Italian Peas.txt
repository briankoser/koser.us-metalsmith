Name: Italian Peas

Ingredients:
- 2 Tbsp. olive oil
- 1 onion, choppped
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 16 oz. frozen peas
- 1 Tbsp. chicken stock
- salt and pepper
- parmesan, to garnish

Directions:
- Heat oil in skillet. Stir in garlic and onion; cook about 5 minutes. Add peas and stir in stock; season with salt and pepper. Cover and cook until tender, about 10 minutes. Top with parmesan.

Yield: Serves 6

Comments: You may wish to add more stock, salt and pepper for a little extra flavor.
