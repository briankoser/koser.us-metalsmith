Name: Bacon and Tomato Cups

Tags: snack appetizer party

Ingredients:
- 8 slices bacon, cooked
- 1 tomato, chopped
- 1/2 onion, chopped
- 4 oz. shredded Swiss cheese
- 1/4 c. mayo
- 1 tsp. dried basil
- 30 mini phyllo cups

Directions:
- Grease a mini muffin tin and line with phyllo cups. Stir together all ingredients and spoon into phyllo cups. Bake at 375 degrees for 10-12 minutes.

Yield: 30 cups

Comments: Each cup can be mounded fairly high, as the ingredients will melt down into the cup during baking.
