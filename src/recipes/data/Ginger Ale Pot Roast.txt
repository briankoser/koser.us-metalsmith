Name: Ginger Ale Pot Roast

Tags: dinner

Ingredients:
- 3-lb. sirloin tip roast
- 1/2 c. flour
- 1 envelope dry onion soup mix
- 1 envelope brown gravy mix
- 2 c. ginger ale
- carrots and potatoes (optional)

Directions:
- Coat roast with flour; reserve remaining flour. Place in crockpot. Combine mixes, ginger ale and remaining flour and pour over roast. Cover and cook on High 4-4 1/2 hours. Add carrots and potatoes midway through cook time.

Yield: Serves 4

Comments: A very juicy, tender roast!
