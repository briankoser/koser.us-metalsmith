Name: Vanilla Ice Cream

Ingredients:
- 1 c. whole milk
- 3/4 c. sugar
- pinch of salt
- 2 c. heavy cream
- 1 Tbsp. vanilla extract

Directions:
- In medium bowl, whisk together milk, sugar and salt until sugar is dissolved. Stir in cream and vanilla. Cover and refrigerate 1-2 hours, or overnight. Freeze according to manufacturer's directions.

Yield: Serves 4
