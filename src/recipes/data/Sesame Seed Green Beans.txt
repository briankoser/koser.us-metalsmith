Name: Sesame Seed Green Beans

Tags: side

Ingredients:
- 2 Tbsp. olive oil
- 1 tsp. minced garlic
- 1/8 c. sesame seeds
- 1 (14.5 oz.) can green beans, drained
- salt to taste

Directions:
- Heat oil in skillet. Add remaining ingredients; mix gently until hot.

Yield: 4 servings

Comments: The textures of the seeds and beans contrast nicely.
