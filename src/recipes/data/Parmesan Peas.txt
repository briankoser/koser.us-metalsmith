Name: Parmesan Peas

Ingredients:
- 2 (10 oz.) pkg. frozen green peas
- 3 Tbsp. butter, melted
- 1 Tbsp. lemon juice
- 2 Tbsp. water
- 1/3 c. grated parmesan cheese

Directions:
- Microwave all ingredients except cheese for 6 minutes, stirring once. Let sit for several minutes, then sprinkle with cheese. Serve hot.

Yield: 4-6 servings

Comments: This is probably my favorite way to serve peas.
