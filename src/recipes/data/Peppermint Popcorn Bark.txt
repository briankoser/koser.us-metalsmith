Name: Peppermint Popcorn Bark

Tags: easy dessert party crowd Christmas favorite

Ingredients:
- 2 bags microwave popcorn, plain or butter
- 6 oz. box candy canes, crushed
- 24 oz. pkg. Almond Bark
- 1 tsp. peppermint extract, or a few drops of peppermint oil

Directions:
- Microwave popcorn, then remove unpopped kernels. Place popped corn into large bowl and pour crushed candy canes on top. Melt almond bark according to package directions. Stir in peppermint extract and immediately pour over popcorn, stirring to coat. Pour popcorn onto waxed paper and allow to harden. Break popcorn into smaller chunks and return to bowl for storage.

Yield: 2 bags of popcorn, party-size

Comments: Seriously one of the best Christmas popcorn recipes ever!
