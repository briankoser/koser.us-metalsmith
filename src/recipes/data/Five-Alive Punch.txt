Name: Five-Alive Punch

Tags: easy beverage crowd party

Ingredients:
- 2 cans frozen Five-Alive juice concentrate
- 1 can pink lemonade concentrate
- 1 (2 liter) bottle ginger ale, chilled

Directions:
- Dilute frozen concentrates according to can directions and mix in large punch bowl. When ready to serve, add ginger ale.

Yield: 32-36 punch cups

Comments: This might not be super pretty to look at, but it makes good punch. I served it as "grog" on the night of our pirate-themed mystery dinner.
