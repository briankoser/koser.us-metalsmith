Name: South African Milktart

Tags: breakfast dessert easy favorite

Ingredients:
- 3 Tbsp. butter, melted
- 1 c. sugar
- 3 eggs, separated
- 1 c. *cake flour
- 1 tsp. baking powder
- 1/4 tsp. salt
- 1 tsp. vanilla
- 4 c. milk
- 1 Tbsp. cinnamon sugar

Directions:
- Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
- Grease a 9" *deep* pie plate. In large bowl, mix butter and sugar until smooth; add egg yolks. Add flour, baking powder, salt, vanilla and milk; mix.
- In separate bowl, whip egg whites until stiff; fold into batter. Pour into pie plate and sprinkle with cinnamon sugar.
- Bake for 25 minutes in preheated oven, then reduce temperature to 325 degrees. Continue to bake for 25-30 minutes, or until the center is set when the pie is jiggled. Refrigerate and serve cold (although you may try serving hot).

Yield: 8 slices

Comments: This is good comfort food, and a great way to use up milk that might soon go bad.  *= 2 Tbsp. cornstarch and the rest of a cup of all-purpose flour may be substituted.
