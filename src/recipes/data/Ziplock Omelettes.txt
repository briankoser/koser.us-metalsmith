Name: Ziplock Omelettes

Tags: breakfast easy crowd favorite

Ingredients:
- 2 eggs per Ziplock bag
- anything you would like in an omelette--cheese, mushrooms, peppers, bacon, ham, tomatoes, onions, salsa, spices, hash browns

Directions:
- Crack 2 eggs into a Ziplock bag; massage bag with fingers to "scramble" eggs. Add other ingredients and tightly seal bag.
- Place in large pot of rolling, boiling water for *exactly 13 minutes*. 6-8 can usually be cooked at once. Open the bags and the omelette will roll out easily.

Yield: However many bags you like

Comments: This is a fun, easy meal for guests. Simply set out the ingredients buffet-style and let guests make their own.
