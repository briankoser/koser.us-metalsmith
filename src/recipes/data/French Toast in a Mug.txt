Name: French Toast in a Mug

Tags: easy breakfast

Ingredients:
- 1 Tbsp. butter
- 1/4 c. milk
- 1 egg, beaten
- 1/4 tsp. cinnamon
- 1/2-1 tsp. sugar
- 1/4 tsp. vanilla
- 2 slices bread, torn into pieces

Directions:
- Melt butter in *large* mug, about 30 seconds. Tilt mug to coat all sides with butter. Stir in all remaining ingredients except bread, then press bread into mixture. Sprinkle top with extra cinnamon and sugar. Microwave on High until set, about 90 seconds.

Yield: Serves 1

Comments: Recipe seemed a little bland, but could be tweaked with syrup or sweetened condensed milk. Chopped pecans could also be added.
