Name: Corn Pudding

Tags: easy side Thanksgiving

Ingredients:
- 2 eggs
- 1 c. sugar
- 2 Tbsp. flour
- 1/2 c. milk
- 1 can of cream style corn
- 1/2 Tbsp. vanilla
- 1/2 tsp. cinnamon
- pinch of salt

Directions:
- Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
- Beat eggs in bowl, then add sugar and flour; mix well. Add remaining ingredients, mix and pour into dish.
- Bake for 30 minutes or so until brown.

Yield: Serves 4?

Comments: Growing up, we always had this as part of the main course at Thanksgiving. It is quite sweet, but it adds pleasant variety to the rest of the side dishes.
