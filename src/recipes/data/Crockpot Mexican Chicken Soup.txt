Name: Crockpot Mexican Chicken Soup

Tags: easy meal dinner crowd

Ingredients:
- 2 chicken breasts
- 1 c. chicken broth
- 1 can black beans, drained and rinsed
- 1 can corn, drained (or 1-2 c. frozen corn)
- 1 c. salsa
- 1 package taco seasoning
- 1/2 c. sour cream
- 1/2 c. cheddar cheese
- 1 1/2 c. cooked rice (optional)

Directions:
- Place chicken in crockpot. Top with broth, beans, salsa and taco seasoning. Cover and cook on Low 6 hours (or on High 3 hours). Remove chicken and shred (it may be tender enough to shred right there in the crockpot), then re-add chicken. Stir in sour cream, cheese and rice and serve.

Yield: Serves 4

Comments: Super easy, and very tasty!
