Name: Easy Italian Bagels

Tags: side favorite

Ingredients:
- bagel halves
- butter
- parsley
- Italian seasoning
- parmesan cheese

Directions:
- Spread butter on cut sides of bagels. Sprinkle with herbs (a little sparingly) and parmesan (generously). Broil on High 2-3 minutes or until golden.

Yield: 

Comments: This is a tasty way to throw starch into a meal, and so quick. The amounts are up to the chef's discretion.
