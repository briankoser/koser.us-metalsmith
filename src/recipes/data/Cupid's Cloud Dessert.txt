Name: Cupid's Cloud Dessert

Ingredients:
- 1 c. whipping cream
- 1/4 c. powdered sugar
- 1/2 tsp. vanilla
- 2 c. fresh raspberries

Directions:
- In medium bowl beat whipping cream until light and fluffy. Beat in powdered sugar and vanilla, then gently beat in raspberries (there should be clumps, not whole berries and not shreds). Serve in fancy glasses.

Yield: Serves 4

Comments: Can be made 1-2 hours in advance; cover and refrigerate if doing so.
