Name: Spinach Pasta and Tuna

Ingredients:
- 7 oz. rotini
- 2 Tbsp. butter
- 8 oz. sour cream
- 1 c. shredded cheddar cheese
- 8 oz. shredded Monterey Jack cheese, divided
- 6 oz. fresh spinach
- 2 cans tuna

Directions:
- Cook pasta according to package directions. Drain, then stir in butter until it melts. Grease a slow cooker, then mix sour cream, cheddar cheese, half Monterey Jack and spinach together. Fold in pasta and cook on Low for 1 1/2-2 hours. Before serving, stir in tuna and top with remaining cheese. Serve immediately.

Yield: Serves 3

Comments: Not super fabulous, but not bad and very easy.
