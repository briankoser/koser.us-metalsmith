Name: Upside Down Pizza

Tags: dinner easy

Ingredients:
- 1 lb. ground beef
- 1/2 c. chopped onion
- to taste: garlic powder, italian seasoning, salt and pepper
- 15.5 oz. pizza sauce
- 1 lb. cooked crumbled bacon
- additional desired toppings
- 8-12 oz. grated mozzarella and cheddar
- 2 eggs
- 1 c. milk
- 1 Tbsp. oil
- 1/2 tsp. salt
- 1 c. flour
- parmesan cheese

Directions:
- Brown beef and onion, sprinkling with seasonings while cooking. Drain grease. Mix in pizza sauce, bacon and any other desired toppings. Pour into a greased 9x13" pan and sprinkle with mozzarella and cheddar.* Heat oven to 350 degrees. In small bowl, whisk together eggs, milk, oil, salt and flour. Pour over cheese in pan and sprinkle with parmesan. Bake 30-40 minutes, broiling last minute or two for a crispier crust.

Yield: Feeds 6, 2 slices each

Comments: *Dish can be prepared ahead of time and left in fridge up to this point.
