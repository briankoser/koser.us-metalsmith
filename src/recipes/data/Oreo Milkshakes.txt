Name: Oreo Milkshakes

Ingredients:
- 5 Oreo cookies
- 1 c. milk
- 2 scoops vanilla ice cream
- 1 Tbsp. Hershey's chocolate syrup

Directions:
- Put all ingredients in blender and blend. May garnish with whipped cream and Oreo chunks.

Yield: Serves 2

Comments: You will want to double this recipe, it's so good!
