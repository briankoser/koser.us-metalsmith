Name: Crockpot Caramel Hot Chocolate

Tags: easy crowd party beverage favorite

Ingredients:
- 2 c. semisweet or milk chocolate chips
- 1/2 c. caramels, unwrapped
- 1/8-1/4 c. powdered sugar
- 4 c. milk
- 2 c. heavy cream
- 1 tsp. vanilla

Directions:
- Add all ingredients to crockpot. Cover and cook on Low 2 1/2 hours. Whisk to stir in chocolate and caramel. If desired, top with whipped cream and caramel sauce.

Yield: 6 cups

Comments: I double this recipe when making for a party. Cook time remains the same.
