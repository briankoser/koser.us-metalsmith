Name: Creamy Chicken and Pasta

Ingredients:
- 1 lb. chicken tenders
- to taste: salt, garlic powder, onion powder, cayenne pepper, and parsley
- 2 Tbsp. butter
- 1 c. heavy cream
- 1/2 lb. angel hair pasta, cooked

Directions:
- Sprinkle seasonings on both sides of the chicken. Melt butter in large skillet and add chicken. Cook over medium-high heat until done, about 9 minutes. Add cream to skillet and lower heat; let simmer until sauce thickens, 5-7 minutes. Serve over pasta.

Yield: Feeds 2
