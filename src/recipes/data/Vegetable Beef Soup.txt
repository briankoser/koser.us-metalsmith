Name: Vegetable Beef Soup

Tags: dinner

Ingredients:
- 2 lbs. beef, cut into cubes
- 2 Tbsp. oil
- 4 c. water
- 2 carrots, diced
- 2 stalks celery, diced
- 1 small onion, chopped
- 1 1/2 c. diced tomatoes
- 1/2 c. peas
- 1/2 c. corn
- 1 can green beans
- 5 beef bouillon cubes
- 2 tsp. salt
- 1/2 tsp. parsley
- 1/4 tsp. each oregano, thyme, pepper
- 2 bay leaves
- dash of sage
- 3 medium potatoes, diced

Directions:
- Brown meat in oil in large Dutch oven. Add all remaining ingredients except potatoes and simmer 1 hour. Add potatoes and simmer 1 more hour. Discard bay leaves and serve.

Yield: Serves about 8

Comments: This is a VERY hearty soup, so a little goes a long way. It's also flexible, as you can omit any veggies you don't have (but the more the better!).
