Name: Peanut Butter Cup Ice Cream

Tags: dessert favorite

Ingredients:
- 1 c. peanut butter (not natural)
- 2/3 c. sugar
- 1 c. whole milk
- 2 c. heavy cream
- 1 tsp. vanilla extract
- roughly 1 c. chopped Reese's peanut butter cups

Directions:
- In medium bowl, use hand mixer on low speed to combine peanut butter and sugar until smooth. Add milk and mix on low until sugar is dissolved, 1-2 minutes. Stir in heavy cream and vanilla. Cover and refrigerate 1-2 hours, or overnight. Freeze according to manufacturer's directions. During last 5 minutes of freezing, add Reese's peanut butter cups.

Yield: Serves 4

Comments: DO NOT use more than one cup of peanut butter, or this ice cream will be overwhelmingly thick.
