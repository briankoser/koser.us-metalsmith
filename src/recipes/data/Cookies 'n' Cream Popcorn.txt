Name: Cookies 'n' Cream Popcorn

Tags: easy dessert snack crowd party

Ingredients:
- 3 bags popcorn
- 20 Oreos, finely crushed
- 24 oz. white vanilla almond bark

Directions:
- Pop popcorn and pour into large bowl, removing unpopped kernels. Melt almond bark according to package directions until melted and smooth. Drizzle over popcorn and sprinkle with Oreo crumbs. Stir and serve!

Yield: party size

Comments: Very tasty!
