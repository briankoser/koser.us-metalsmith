Name: Swiss Chicken

Tags: easy dinner crowd favorite

Ingredients:
- 8 boneless skinless chicken breast halves
- 1 can cream of chicken soup
- 1/3 cup water
- 8 slices Swiss cheese
- 2 cups Italian bread crumbs
- 1/2 cup butter

Directions:
- Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
- In 9x13" dish layer chicken, then soup, then water; top each chicken with Swiss cheese and breadcrumbs. Cut butter into pads and place over top of casserole. Bake for 45 minutes; let cool a little and serve. 

Yield: Serves 8
