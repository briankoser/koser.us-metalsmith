Name: Peppermint Candy Cane Shake

Ingredients:
- 3 c. vanilla ice cream
- 1 1/2 c. milk
- 1/2 tsp. peppermint extract
- 1 Tbsp. crushed candy canes
- whipped cream and 1 tsp. crushed candy cane (optional garnishs)

Directions:
- In blender combine ice cream and milk until smooth. Add peppermint extract and 1 Tbsp. candy canes and blend a little more. Pour into glasses and top with garnishes.

Yield: 2 large shakes

Comments: Very tasty and easy!
