Name: Spinach and Tomato Tortellini Soup

Tags: dinner meal

Ingredients:
- 1 1/2 Tbsp. olive oil
- 1 1-1/2 c. finely chopped onion
- 6 cloves garlic, minced (more or less)
- 4 c. chicken broth
- 28 oz. diced canned tomatoes
- salt and pepper to taste
- 20 oz. package three cheese tortellini
- 1 tsp. sugar
- 6-9 oz. fresh spinach, chopped
- 3 Tbsp. chopped fresh basil
- grated Parmesan cheese (garnish)

Directions:
- Over medium heat, saute onion in olive oil until tender; add garlic and saute 30 seconds longer. Add chicken broth, tomatoes with juice, salt and pepper and bring to a boil. Add tortellini and let boil 7 minutes (or longer if directed on tortellini package). Stir in sugar, spinach and basil and cook until heated through (spinach should wilt). Serve into bowls and garnish with Parmesan cheese.

Yield: Serves 6
