Name: Cheesy Chicken and Rice Bake

Tags: easy dinner meal

Ingredients:
- 1 can cream of chicken soup
- 1 1/3 c. water
- 3/4 c. uncooked long-grain rice
- 1/2 tsp. onion powder
- 1/4 tsp. black pepper
- 1 bag frozen whole veggies from Sam's
- 4-6 skin/boneless chicken breasts, *thawed*
- fresh ground pepper and shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
- Stir together first 6 ingredients in 8x12" dish. Top with chicken and sprinkle with fresh ground pepper. Cover with foil and bake at 375 degrees for 45 minutes. Uncover and sprinkle with Cheddar.

Yield: 4-6 servings

Comments: Be liberal with pepper and cheese--they contrast nicely and give good pop. This is a good all-in-one meal!
