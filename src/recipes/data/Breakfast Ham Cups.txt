Name: Breakfast Ham Cups

Tags: appetizer easy breakfast

Ingredients:
- 1 package sliced ham (not shredded), about 10 slices
- 1/4 c. chopped onion
- 1/4 c. chopped bell pepper (any color)
- 2 Tbsp. butter
- 5 eggs
- 4 Tbsp. milk
- salt and pepper to taste
- 2-4 oz. shredded cheese
- 1-2 green onions, chopped

Directions:
- Grease muffin cups and heat oven to 350 degrees. Gently line each cup with one slice of ham. In microwaveable bowl place onion, bell pepper and butter; microwave until cooked, 1-2 minutes. Meanwhile, beat together eggs, milk, salt and pepper. Set aside. Evenly divide onions and bell pepper between muffin cups, then add cheese. Pour milk mixture on top (about 1/2 egg per cup) and sprinkle with green onions. Bake 20-25 minutes and serve.

Yield: 10 ham cups

Comments: Very tasty, easy and flexible--you can add whatever you like to these.
