Name: Italian Chicken Pasta

Ingredients:
- 7 chicken breast halves
- 2 c. Italian salad dressing
- 16 oz. uncooked rigatoni
- 1/4 c. butter
- 28 oz. diced tomatoes with Italian herbs
- 1/4 tsp. red pepper
- 1 Tbsp. dried parsley
- parmesan cheese

Directions:
- Dice chicken and place in Ziploc bag; add Italian dressing and marinate at least 30 minutes. Meanwhile, cook pasta according to package directions and drain.
- Melt butter in large pot over medium heat. Scoop chicken into pot, allowing some marinade to go with it; discard remaining marinade. Add diced tomatoes and cook 15 minutes or so, seasoning with red pepper and parsley. Serve over pasta and sprinkle with cheese.

Yield: Serves 6

Comments: Serve with crusty bread.
