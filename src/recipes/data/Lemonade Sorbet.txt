Name: Lemonade Sorbet

Tags: dessert

Ingredients:
- 1 1/2 c. cold water
- 1 c. frozen lemonade concentrate, thawed
- 3 Tbsp. honey (you may wish to use less for a more tart flavor)

Directions:
- Blend all ingredients in food processor until smooth. Freeze about 4 hours until firm, stirring several times to keep mixture smooth.

Yield: 4 servings

Comments: This is not a wow-factor like the raspberry sorbet, but it is still very refreshing and just the thing for a hot day.
