Name: Cream of Tomato Soup

Ingredients:
- 28 oz. can whole or diced tomatoes, with juice
- 1/2 c. chopped onion
- 1 Tbsp. dried parsley
- 1 tsp. sugar
- 1/2 tsp. basil
- 1/4 tsp. pepper
- 4 Tbsp. butter
- 3 Tbsp. flour
- 2 c. chicken broth
- 1 c. milk
- salt to taste

Directions:
- In saucepan combine tomatoes, onion, parsley, sugar, basil and pepper. Bring to a boil and reduce heat. Cover and simmer 20 minutes.
- Pour hot mixture into blender and blend until smooth, about 30 seconds.
- Melt butter in saucepan. Stir in broth and flour and bring to a boil, stirring constantly. Add blended tomato mixture and simmer 5 minutes. Stir in milk and salt and cook 1 minute longer.

Yield: 4 servings

Comments: Brian never liked tomato soup until I made this. It goes very nicely with buttered toast, or best of all, my Homemade Cheesy Garlic Croutons.
