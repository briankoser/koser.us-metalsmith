Name: BLT Pasta Salad

Tags: side party favorite crowd

Ingredients:
- 1 lb. bow tie pasta
- 1 lb. cooked bacon
- 1 large ripe tomato, chopped
- 10 leaves of lettuce, torn into pieces
- 1 small onion, chopped (optional)
- 1 c. zesty italian salad dressing
- 1/2 c. buttermilk ranch dressing
- salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
- Cook pasta according to package directions. Drain, cool and stir in all remaining ingredients.

Yield: Serves 6+
