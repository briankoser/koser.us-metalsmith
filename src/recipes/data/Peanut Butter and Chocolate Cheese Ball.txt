Name: Peanut Butter and Chocolate Cheese Ball

Ingredients:
- 1 (8 oz.) pkg. cream cheese, softened
- 1/4 c. butter, softened
- 1/4 c. creamy peanut butter
- 3/4 c. powdered sugar
- 2 Tbsp. brown sugar
- 3 Tbsp. cocoa
- 1/2 c. each of chocolate chips and peanut butter chips
- 2/3 c. finely chopped pecans
- graham crackers, to serve 

Directions:
- With electric mixer beat cream cheese, butter and peanut butter at medium speed until creamy. Stir all remaining ingredients except pecans and graham crackers; cover and refrigerate 2 hours.
- Shape chilled mixture into a ball, then roll in pecans. Serve on graham crackers.

Yield: 4" ball

Comments: This so yummy! Definitely something you have to serve when guests come over, as it's very sweet.
