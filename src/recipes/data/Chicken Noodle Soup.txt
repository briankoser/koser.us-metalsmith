Name: Chicken Noodle Soup

Ingredients:
- 12 c. chicken stock
- 5-6 chicken breasts, cooked and diced
- about 6 stalks of celery, chopped
- chopped carrots to taste
- 4 cubes chicken bouillon
- to taste: salt, pepper, poultry seasoning, garlic powder
- 1 bay leaf
- 8-12 oz. cooked noodles

Directions:
- Combine all ingredients except noodles in large pot. Heat to boiling and let cook 15-30 minutes, reducing heat halfway through. During last two minutes of cooking, add noodles. Remove bay leaf and serve.

Yield: Serves at least 6

Comments: If you have any large chicken or turkey bones, throw them in the pot for extra flavor.
