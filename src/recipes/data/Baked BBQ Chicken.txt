Name: Baked BBQ Chicken

Tags: dinner

Ingredients:
- 2-3 lbs. chicken pieces
- 1 c. ketchup
- 1/4 c. water
- 1/4 c. vinegar (or 1/8 c. balsamic vinegar)
- 1/4 c. butter
- 3 Tbsp. brown sugar
- 2 Tbsp. Worcestershire sauce
- 1 tsp. dry mustard
- 1/2 tsp. paprika
- 1/4 tsp. pepper
- 1 1/2 tsp. salt
- 1 1/2 tsp. lemon
- chopped onion

Directions:
- Heat oven to 350 degrees.
- In a saucepan combine all ingredients except chicken. Bring to a boil and simmer for 10 minutes.
- Place chicken pieces in baking dish and spoon sauce over them; add 1/4 c. water. Bake uncovered for 2 hours, basting and turning occasionally.

Yield: 3-4 servings

Comments: This is one of the few BBQ recipes I like.
