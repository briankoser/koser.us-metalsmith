Name: Creamy Shrimp Pasta

Tags: seafood dinner

Ingredients:
- 1 stick butter
- 1 lb. raw shrimp
- 1-2 tsp. minced garlic, or garlic powder to taste
- 1-2 Tbsp. parsley
- 1-2 Tbsp. basil
- salt and pepper to taste
- 1-2 Tbsp. lemon juice
- 1/3 block softened cream cheese
- 1 can diced tomatoes with juice
- 1/2 lb. angel hair pasta, cooked and drained
- parmesan cheese for topping

Directions:
- Melt butter in large skillet. Add shrimp, all seasonings and lemon juice and cook until shrimp are pink and opaque. Stir in cream cheese and tomatoes and let simmer a few minutes to blend. Serve over pasta and top with parmesan.

Yield: Serves 2-3

Comments: This dish is intentionally very liquid. You can stretch the dish by using a whole pound of pasta, but the flavor will be much less intense.
