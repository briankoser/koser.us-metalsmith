Name: Mom Koser's Taco Dip

Ingredients:
- 2 (8 oz.) pkgs. cream cheese
- 1 (16 oz.) container sour cream
- 1 pkg. Taco seasoning
- finely chopped onion
- chopped tomatoes
- shredded lettuce
- shredded cheddar cheese
- sliced black olives (optional)

Directions:
- Blend together cream cheese and sour cream. Sprinkle remaining ingredients on top and serve with tortilla chips.

Yield: 

Comments: The amounts for the onion, tomato, lettuce and cheese are dependent on the cook.
