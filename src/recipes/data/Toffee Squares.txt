Name: Toffee Squares

Ingredients:
- saltine crackers (about 35)
- 1 cup butter
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 1/2 cups semi-sweet chocolate chips

Directions:
- Pre-heat oven to 375 degrees.
- Lay crackers in cookie sheet.
- Melt butter on stovetop; mix in sugar well.
- Drizzle butter and sugar mixture evenly onto crackers.
- Bake in oven until golden (about 10 minutes).
- Sprinkle chocolate chips over crackers; spread when melted.
- Let cool for 20 minutes.
- Chill in fridge for 1 hour.

Yield: About 35 pieces

Comments: This is a cheat to get good toffee with little effort. :)
