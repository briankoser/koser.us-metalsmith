Name: Creamy Green Beans

Tags: easy side

Ingredients:
- 3/8 c. milk
- 4 oz. cream cheese
- 1/4 tsp. garlic powder
- 1/4 c. grated parmesan cheese
- 2 (15 oz.) cans green beans, drained

Directions:
- In saucepan, combine all ingredients and heat until cheeses melt. Stir in beans and heat through, then serve.

Yield: 4-6 servings

Comments: This recipe is easily doubled to serve larger groups.
