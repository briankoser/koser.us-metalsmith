Name: Crockpot Garlic Parmesan Potatoes

Ingredients:
- 3 lb. baby red potatoes, halved
- 2 Tbsp. olive oil
- 2 Tbsp. melted butter
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 1/2 tsp. each: dried oregano, basil and dill
- 2 tsp. dried parsley
- salt and pepper to taste
- 1/4 c. grated parmesan cheese

Directions:
- Lightly grease the inside of a crockpot. Place everything except cheese in crockpot and stir. Cover and cook until tender on Low 4-5 hours, or High 2-3 hours. Just before serving add cheese and stir.

Yield: 8 servings

Comments: This is perfect for a church potluck!
