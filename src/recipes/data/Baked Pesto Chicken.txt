Name: Baked Pesto Chicken

Tags: easy dinner

Ingredients:
- 4-6 boneless skinless chicken breasts
- 1/2 c. pesto
- 1 sliced tomato
- 1-2 c. shredded mozzarella
- 4 c. cooked rice

Directions:
- Heat oven to 400 degrees. Place chicken in 8x12" baking dish. Spread pesto on chicken and top with tomato slices. Sprinkle with cheese and bake 30-40 minutes, until juices run clear. Serve over cooked rice.

Yield: Serves 4

Comments: Fabulously easy and tasty!
