Name: Mojito Fruit Salad

Tags: side fruit party crowd

Ingredients:
- 4 c. chopped watermelon
- 1 lb. strawberries, chopped
- 6 oz. raspberries
- 6 oz. blueberries
- 1/4 c. packed mint, chopped
- 1/4 c. fresh lime juice (about 3 limes)
- 3 Tbsp. powdered sugar

Directions:
- In large bowl add watermelon, strawberries, raspberries, blueberries and mint. In small separate bowl stir together lime juice and powdered sugar. Pour juice over fruit and gently toss with a spatula. Cover and let sit in fridge at least 15 minutes to allow juices to blend.

Yield: Serves 6-8

Comments: Great for a summer picnic or potluck!
