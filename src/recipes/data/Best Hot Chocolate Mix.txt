Name: Best Hot Chocolate Mix

Ingredients:
- 1 box (8 qt.) (10 2/3 c.) nonfat dry milk powder
- 1 medium jar (16 oz.) (4 c.) creamer
- 1 box (1 lb.) (4 1/2 c.) powdered sugar
- 2 lb. box (5 c.) Nestle Quick chocolate powder
- 1/2 bag (1 c.) chocolate chips
- 1/2 bag (1 c.) white chocolate chips
- 1 bag (10 1/2 c.) mini marshmallows
- 10 peppermint candies, crushed

Directions:
- Mix first four ingredients together. Add last four ingredients and mix. Use 1/3 cup mix to 8 oz. hot water or milk.

Yield: About 70 servings

Comments: The name says it all.
