Name: Crab Mac 'n' Cheese

Ingredients:
- 1 lb. macaroni
- 1 tsp. salt
- 2 Tbsp. flour
- 1 Tbsp. Dijon mustard
- 1/2 tsp. pepper
- 2 eggs
- 1/2 c. sour cream
- 12 oz. evaporated milk
- 8 oz. shredded cheddar cheese
- 10 oz. crab meat

Directions:
- Cook macaroni according to package directions; drain and set aside. Grease 9x13" casserole dish and heat oven to 350 degrees. In large bowl combine salt, flour, mustard, pepper, sour cream, eggs and milk; whisk very well. Layer half the macaroni in casserole dish, half the cheese and half the crab; repeat layers. Pour flour mixture on top and bake uncovered 30 minutes.

Yield: Serves 6

Comments: Very good! I like to serve it with extra salt and pepper.
