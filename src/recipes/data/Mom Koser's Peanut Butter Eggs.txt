Name: Mom Koser's Peanut Butter Eggs

Egg Ingredients:
- 1/2 c. butter, softened
- 8 oz. cream cheese
- 2/3 c. condensed milk
- 6 c. powdered sugar
- 1 tsp. vanilla
- 1/2 tsp. salt
- 1 1/4 c. peanut butter

Coating Ingredients:
- 4 oz. semisweet chocolate chips
- 4 oz. unsweetened chocolate
- 1 bar German chocolate
- 1/2 cake of paraffin wax

Directions:
- Using mixer, combine all ingredients for eggs except peanut butter. Add peanut butter; refrigerate until stiffened and shape into eggs (use powdered sugar to coat hands as needed to keep egg mixture from sticking to you). Chill again.
- Melt coating ingredients in double boiler. Coat eggs in chocolate mixture, using a large paperclip with 1 end unfolded. Cool on waxed paper, then store in refrigerator.
