Name: Mom Koser's Basic White Sauce

Ingredients:
- 1 Tbsp. butter
- 1 Tbsp. flour
- dash of pepper, more if desired
- 3/4 c. milk

Directions:
- Melt butter. Add the rest of the ingredients. Over medium-high heat, cook and stir constantly with wire whisk until thick and bubbly. Over medium-low, cook 1 minute more, continuing to stir. Can add 3/4 cup of cheese if desired.

Yield:
