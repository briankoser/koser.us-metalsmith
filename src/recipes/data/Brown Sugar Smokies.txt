Name: Brown Sugar Smokies

Tags: appetizer crowd party

Ingredients:
- *lots* of brown sugar
- 1 lb. bacon, cut into thirds
- smokies, or small sausages

Directions:
- Dredge bacon and smokies in sugar, then roll up smokies in bacon. Cover a plate with paper towels and place smokies seam side down on plate. Microwave on High 2 minutes. Place in 9x13"pan and bake at 350 degrees for 10 minutes. Top with more brown sugar and broil on High for 5 minutes.

Yield: 

Comments: This is a fabulous appetizer that will be eaten up quickly at any function.
