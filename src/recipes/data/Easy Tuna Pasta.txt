Name: Easy Tuna Pasta

Ingredients:
- 12 oz. macaroni or elbows
- 1/2 c. butter
- 1/2 c. olive oil
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 can condensed chicken broth
- 2-3 tsp. dried basil
- 2 cans tuna
- grated parmesan cheese

Directions:
- Cook and drain pasta. In large saucepan, heat butter and oil and cook garlic until golden. Add broth and basil; heat to boiling, then reduce heat to low. Simmer uncovered 10 minutes. Add tuna with liquid and simmer 2 minutes more, stirring gently to break up meat. Stir in pasta and top with cheese.

Yield: Serves 4

Comments: Nothing fancy, but not bad, and very easy to put together.
