Name: Easy Chowder

Tags: dinner

Ingredients:
- 8 slices bacon
- 1 c. chopped onion
- 2 cans cream of potato or celery
- 3 c. milk
- 2 cans (8 oz. each) diced carrots (perhaps fresh?)
- 2 c. sliced smokies
- 2-4 tsp. dried parsley

Directions:
- Fry bacon until crisp; remove to paper towel and crumble. Cook onion in bacon drippings until tender. In large pot, combine all ingredients (except bacon drippings). Heat through, stirring occasionally.

Yield: Serves 4
