Name: Baked Apples

Tags: dessert fruit

Ingredients:
- 1 Granny Smith apple
- 1 tsp. chopped pecans or walnuts
- 1 Tbsp. brown sugar
- 1 tsp. raisins
- 1 Tbsp. butter
- 1 3" cinnamon stick
- whipped cream

Directions:
- Preheat oven to 375 degrees. Cut the top off apple; core and seed it, but do not cut through to the bottom.
- Fill apple with nuts, sugar and raisins; dot with butter. Wedge cinnamon stick in the middle.
- Butter a pie dish; fill with 1/4" water and place apples in dish. Bake until apples are soft but still retain their shape (about 25 minutes). Serve with whipped cream.

Yield: 1 apple per person

Comments: This is just the thing for a cold night! I can't describe the home-cooked goodness of this recipe--you just have to taste it for yourself.
