Name: Applesauce Cake

Tags: dessert

Cake Ingredients:
- 2 1/2 c. flour
- 2 c. sugar
- 1 1/2 tsp. each of baking soda and salt
- 1/4  tsp. baking powder
- 2/4 tsp. ground cinnamon
- 1/2 tsp. ground cloves
- 1/2 tsp. ground allspice
- 1 1/2 c. applesauce
- 1/2 c. each of water and shortening
- 2 eggs
- 1 c. each of raisins and chopped walnuts

Frosting Ingredients:
- 2/3 c. packed brown sugar
- 1/4 c. butter
- 1/2 c. chopped nuts
- 2 Tbsp. milk

Directions:
- Heat oven to 350 degrees. Grease and flour 9x13" pan. 
- Beat all ingredients (except raisins and walnuts) on Low for 30 seconds, then on High for 3 min. Stir in raisins and walnuts.
- Pour into pan and bake 60-65 minutes.
- Mix together all frosting ingredients; spread on warm cake and broil for about 3 minutes or until frosting bubbles. Watch closely!

Yield: 12 pieces
