Name: Parmesan Corn

Tags: easy side favorite

Ingredients:
- 1 pkg. frozen corn
- to taste: butter, parmesan cheese, Italian seasoning and salt

Directions:
- Cook corn according to package directions; drain water and pour corn into bowl. Stir in remaining ingredients, adding amounts of each until dish has an irresistible flavor.

Yield: 

Comments: This is Brian's favorite corn recipe! I usually dislike recipes that don't give specific amounts, but this one really depends on the cook's taste; plus, it allows you flexibility when making greater or lesser amounts of corn.
