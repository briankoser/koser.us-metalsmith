Name: Baleadas

Ingredients:
- large tortillas
- refried beans
- scrambled eggs, 2 per person
- shredded cheese
- sour cream

Directions:
- Spread beans on tortilla and add eggs; top with cheese and microwave until cheese is melted. Top with sour cream and serve.

Yield: 

Comments: Baleadas are a Honduran staple, their basic sandwich. We added the cheese to Americanize it. This is a very simple meal, but it's a good one for when you're in a hurry.
