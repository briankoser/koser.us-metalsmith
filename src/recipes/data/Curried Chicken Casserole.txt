Name: Curried Chicken Casserole

Tags: easy dinner meal favorite

Ingredients:
- 3 cooked chicken breasts
- 1 large bag of broccoli, frozen or fresh
- 2 cans cream of chicken soup
- 1/2 c. mayonnaise
- 1/2 lb. cheddar, grated
- curry powder to taste
- seasoned bread crumbs or croutons to taste
- 5-6 c. cooked rice

Directions:
- Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
- Cut chicken into bite-size pieces. Cook broccoli, drain, and put in bottom of 8x12" casserole dish. Place chicken on top.
- Mix soup, mayo and cheddar; add curry powder to taste. Spread over the chicken and sprinkle with bread crumbs. Cover with foil and bake 30-40 minutes. Uncover and bake another 10-15 minutes. Let stand about 10 minutes before serving. Serve over rice.

Yield: Serves 6
