Name: Crockpot Chicken Parmesan Soup

Tags: easy dinner

Ingredients:
- 3 garlic cloves, minced
- 3/4 green bell pepper, chopped
- 14.5 oz. diced tomatoes
- 1/2 lb. boneless, skinless chicken breasts
- 3 c. chicken broth
- 1/2 c. chopped onion
- 1/2 c. parmesan cheese, plus extra for garnish
- 1 tsp. dried basil
- 2 tsp. dried oregano
- 1/8 tsp. red pepper
- 4 oz. uncooked spiral pasta

Directions:
- In crockpot combine all ingredients except pasta. Cover and cook on High 3 1/2 hours, or on Low 7 hours. Shred chicken inside crockpot using a fork. Add pasta, cover and cook on High 30 minutes until pasta is cooked.

Yield: Serves 6

Comments: This goes beautifully with homemade bread and butter.
